These rules are disclosed to clarify the various responsibilities of all community members at Evolution. They have been compiled and are maintained regularly by Staff Team and should be adhered to by everyone.

Section 1.0 Spawn Rules

Last Updated 29 Aug by Liam Krage

Civilians may not CAMP spawns

Police and Civilian spawns are green zones

This means there is no crime in this area what so ever, if a crime starts elsewhere and proceeds into the green zone, it may continue. This counts for gang bases also.

.

This means no civilians may be zip tied, stunned, shot or run over in these zones by other players.

Cops are free to arrest anyone in a green zone, but your gang members may not initiate on the cops to assist you

You may not evade roleplay in a green zone/spawn zone.

You cannot flee into a spawn zone/greenzone to avoid any type of roleplay.

You may not play music in a spawn zone

Playing music of any sort in a spawn zone is not allowed.

Section 3.0 Vehicle Death Match (VDM)

Last Updated 05 Aug by SLT

Intentionally running people over is prohibited no matter what reason you had, You may, however, ram a person vehicle if you have a roleplay reason to do so.

You may not stand behind or in front of a vehicle when you are robbing the person in said vehicle, this is using the rule against VDM for your benefit and advantage.

If you accidentally run over a player please report it to a member of staff straight away and explain to them why you did it. They will then solve the issue without giving you a punishment.

Mass VDM (four or more) will result in an instant two-week ban from the server.

Section 4.0 Random Death Match (RDM)

Last Updated 05 Aug by SLT

Intentionally running people over is prohibited no matter what reason you had, You may, however, ram a person vehicle if you have a roleplay reason to do so.

You may only kill police officers if they:

  • Shoot at you first.
  • Are arresting a fellow gang member (You must still initiate).
  • Are not complying with your demands in a hostage situation.
  • Are inside your gang base (You must still initiate).
  • Are arresting you.
  • Are chasing you *If you are wanted*.

You may only kill civilians if they:

  • Shoot at you first.
  • Are attempting to kidnap/rob you or your fellow officers.
  • Are not complying with your demands.
  • Are attacking your fellow officer.
  • Are arresting you.
  • Are inside restricted areas (You must still initiate.)

Section 5.0 New Life Rule (NLR)

Last Updated 05 Aug by SLT

When you are killed by a player, police officer or anything natural you must forget all the things within your past life and you may not return to the place of your death until fifteen minutes are up. This also means you can not go back to take your vehicle that you used in your previous life until the fifteen minutes are up.

It is not a New Life if you die by a glitch, so you may return to the place you died.

A death that is caused by somebody shooting at you when you were in cover (DeSync) is not a glitch.

If you are revived by an ESU member you forget the way you died and the people involved however you may continue with your life.

If you were a victim of RDM, with a staff member’s permission you may return to your Body/Items/Vehicles.

Police officers may return to the scene of their death if they are called for backup to that location by a living officer.

Gang Members may not call for back up, and players may not return to the scene in which they died at for any reason unless the fifteen minutes are up.

However, if they died in a chase and the chase moved from the location of their death they are allowed to assist their gang member(s).

Section 6.0 Gang Rules and Gang Wars

Last Updated 05 Aug by SLT

Only members of the same whitelisted gang can initiate a major crime. This means that having one representative from each gang and initiating a major crime then bringing in more gangs into the scenario is now against the rules.

If a whitelisted gang wishes to patrol in a group larger than four then they must appoint at least one head gang member. From this, if whilst in a group larger than four a member of the gang receives warning points for whatever reason then the head gang member that they are patrolling with will automatically receive equivalent warning points.

Gang members may assist other gang members if they are being arrested/kidnapped or if they are in a firefight.

Gangs can KOS any unauthorised personnel inside their gang base.

If your gang member is restrained/arrested, you have no communication with them so they can’t call you to assist.

If your gang member has been taken hostage and their trackers and communication devices have been removed you may not communicate with your gang member whatsoever.

Gang Wars may only happen if there is a legit roleplay reason, like taking a gang member hostage.

During a Gang War, you may shoot the enemy gang at any given time if you have identified them (You must still initiate).

Gang members may not fight within a green zone. This rule does not count with gang base(s).

Section 8.0 Fail RP Rules

Last Updated 05 Aug by SLT

Punching civilians to death inside of prison cells of the Department of Corrections is strictly prohibited, breaking this rule will result in FailRP warning points.

FailRP is when a player does something to avoid roleplay.

When you are being arrested/transported to jail, You may not get out of the police officers vehicle or get into the driver seat.

If you are tased out of your car by a police officer, you may not get back into your car.

If you disconnect/respawn while handcuffed or restrained this is considered FailRP.

If you are handcuffed or zip tied and you either get released out of restraints for an excessive amount of restrainment time or jump into a vehicle this is considered FailRP!

If you are restrained/handcuffed and are put inside a vehicle you may not switch to the driver seat and drive away as this is FailRP.

Jumping out of the vehicle whilst it is being unlocked is FailRP.

Jumping in cars as the person unlocks them is not allowed.

Jumping into peoples driver seat when they unlock the cars is not allowed.

Landing Rotary or Fixed Wing Aircraft on roads or in the city without declaring a roleplay reason via text message / radio to the police first is considered FailRP.

You may only send people to jail from the Department of Corrections.

In order to initiate the act of roleplay, you must be the person doing it or a member of your gang must do it on your behalf, you cannot send a random player or friend to initiate.

Police officers may shoot civilians/vehicles if they have opened fire upon the officer first or if they are declared code red by the appropriately ranked officer(s).

Police must always remove criminal evidence e.g. weapons from crime scenes.

Police may never switch to civilian and use their police car and weapons they had.

Police officers may use lethal after fifteen minutes of a chase.

Police officers may use lethal after fifteen minutes of a chase.

Police officers are not allowed to camp any illegal areas or the bank.

Police Officers may never ask a civilian to perform any illegal act.
